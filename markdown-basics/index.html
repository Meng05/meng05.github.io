Blockquotes

Blockquotes can contain multiple paragraphs. Add a > on the blank lines between the paragraphs.

Tomorrow is another day!

Today is a good day!

1
2
3

>Tomorrow is another day!
>
>Today is a good day!

Nested Blockquotes:

Tomorrow is another day!

Today is a good day!

1
2
3

> Tomorrow is another day!
>
>> Today is a good day!
